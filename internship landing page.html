Internship Program Overview

At Cognifyz Technologies, we are offering an exciting Web Developer Internship where you will work closely with our team to build innovative web applications. The internship lasts for 3 to 6 months and offers invaluable real-world experience in web development, coding, and problem-solving.

Internship Highlights

  • Gain real-world experience working on live projects.
  • Receive mentorship and guidance from experienced developers.
  • Opportunity to build a portfolio that showcases your work.
  • Flexible work hours and the option to work remotely.
  • Potential full-time employment upon successful completion of the internship.

Desired Skills & Technologies

We are looking for interns who have a passion for web development and are eager to learn. Ideal candidates should have experience or knowledge in the following:

  • HTML, CSS, and JavaScript (Vanilla JS and ES6+)
  • Experience with web development frameworks like React.js or Vue.js
  • Basic understanding of version control (Git and GitHub)
  • Responsive web design principles
  • Familiarity with APIs and AJAX
  • Strong communication skills and a team-oriented attitude
